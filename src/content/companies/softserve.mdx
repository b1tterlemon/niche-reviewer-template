# Is SoftServe the right AI agent development company for you?

SoftServe is a well-suited partner for mid-market and enterprise organisations that need generative AI and AI agent delivery alongside broader IT services — particularly if they are already working in healthcare, SaaS, or high-tech verticals where SoftServe has a documented delivery record. It is not the right choice for teams that want a pure-play AI partner or a small boutique specialising exclusively in agent development.

## What services does SoftServe offer for AI agent development?

SoftServe's AI capabilities are delivered through a dedicated generative AI practice embedded within a broader IT services organisation of over 10,000 engineers. Core offerings include custom AI agent development, RAG pipelines, cloud-native AI integration, and MLOps infrastructure.

**Core service areas:**
- **Custom AI agents:** Design and build of single-purpose agents and conversational systems using LangChain, OpenAI APIs, and Azure AI.
- **RAG pipeline development:** Document ingestion, embedding, and retrieval architectures for enterprise knowledge bases using vector databases.
- **MLOps and model deployment:** Infrastructure for deploying, monitoring, and updating LLM-based systems at scale on AWS Bedrock and Azure AI.
- **Enterprise AI integration:** Connecting agentic systems to existing data platforms, ERP systems, and business applications.
- **AI consulting and architecture:** Roadmap development, build-vs-buy assessment, and agentic system design reviews.

## What are SoftServe's key strengths in AI agent development?

SoftServe's strengths lie in combining a mature delivery organisation with a dedicated and growing generative AI practice. Based on publicly available information:

- **Healthcare specialisation:** SoftServe has documented delivery experience in US healthcare IT, including AI-driven document processing and clinical workflow automation — a vertical with stringent compliance requirements that not all AI dev shops can serve.
- **Cloud platform partnerships:** AWS, Azure, and GCP partner status means SoftServe teams are certified on the managed AI services (Bedrock, Azure OpenAI, Vertex AI) that enterprise clients typically mandate.
- **Delivery centre diversity:** Engineering teams span Ukraine, Poland, Bulgaria, and North America. This provides time-zone flexibility for US and European clients.
- **Scale without enterprise lock-in:** At 10,000+ employees, SoftServe can scale teams without the process overhead of an EPAM or Infosys, while still offering the governance expected by mid-market enterprise buyers.

## How much does SoftServe charge for AI agent development?

SoftServe does not publish rates. Pricing is scoped per project after an initial discovery phase. Engagement models include dedicated teams, retainer agreements, and T&M arrangements. Based on market benchmarks for Eastern European delivery centres, blended rates are typically below major US or UK consulting firms, though higher than pure offshore providers.

| Engagement model | How it works | Typical fit |
|---|---|---|
| Dedicated team | Allocated SoftServe engineers, typically 4–12 per team | Sustained AI product development or enhancement |
| T&M | Billed against a statement of work, variable hours | Discovery, prototyping, or evolving-scope projects |

## What types of AI agents does SoftServe build?

Based on published case studies and service documentation:

- **Clinical and healthcare agents:** Document processing agents that extract structured data from clinical notes, referrals, and insurance forms.
- **Support automation agents:** LLM-powered agents for tier-1 customer support deflection in SaaS and e-commerce contexts.
- **Data extraction and summarisation agents:** Agents that process large document sets (contracts, reports, research) and surface structured outputs.
- **Internal knowledge retrieval:** RAG-based enterprise knowledge assistants trained on proprietary documentation corpora.

## What should you consider before hiring SoftServe for AI agent development?

SoftServe is a capable partner, but there are specific factors to evaluate before committing:

- **AI as one practice among many:** SoftServe is not AI-only. Its generative AI practice competes for internal attention alongside a large portfolio of non-AI IT services work. Confirm team composition and the seniority of AI engineers assigned to your engagement.
- **Geopolitical delivery risk:** A significant share of SoftServe's engineering capacity is based in Ukraine. For long-term programmes, discuss business continuity and redundancy measures directly.
- **Discovery process:** Rates and timelines are only available after scoping. Budget at least two to four weeks for the initial discovery phase before receiving a firm proposal.

## Who is SoftServe best suited for?

SoftServe is the strongest fit for organisations that:

- Need AI agent development alongside broader IT service delivery (cloud migration, data platform, QA) under a single vendor.
- Operate in healthcare, SaaS, or high-tech verticals where SoftServe has documented delivery experience.
- Want Eastern European delivery economics without engaging a pure offshore provider.
- Have existing AWS or Azure infrastructure that they want to extend with managed AI services.

It is less likely to be the right fit for teams that want a boutique AI-only partner, or for organisations that need on-site delivery in North America.
